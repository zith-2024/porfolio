About Our Service

Crafting Excellence Since 1985

Welcome to ZITH Watch Collection

For over three decades, ZITH Watch Collection has been the premier destination for discerning collectors and enthusiasts seeking the world's most prestigious timepieces. We pride ourselves on offering an unparalleled selection of luxury watches from the most renowned manufacturers in the world.

Our Heritage

Founded in 1985, ZITH Watch Collection began as a small boutique with a passion for horological excellence. Today, we stand as one of the most trusted names in luxury timepieces, serving collectors from around the globe.

Curated Selection

Every timepiece in our collection is carefully selected and authenticated by our team of expert horologists. We offer only the finest watches from legendary brands including Rolex, Patek Philippe, Audemars Piguet, and more.

Expert Service

Our dedicated team of watch specialists brings decades of combined experience to help you find the perfect timepiece. From consultation to after-sales service, we're committed to providing an exceptional experience.

What We Offer

Authenticity Guarantee

Every watch is thoroughly inspected and certified authentic by our expert watchmakers. We guarantee the authenticity of every timepiece we sell, giving you complete peace of mind.

Personalized Consultation

Our experienced consultants work one-on-one with you to understand your preferences and help you discover the perfect watch that matches your style and investment goals.

Worldwide Shipping

We ship to collectors worldwide with secure, insured delivery. Your timepiece is carefully packaged and tracked every step of the way to ensure safe arrival.

Premium After-Sales Service

Our relationship doesn't end at purchase. We offer comprehensive servicing, maintenance, and support for all watches in our collection, ensuring your investment lasts a lifetime.

Trade-In Program

Looking to upgrade your collection? We offer competitive trade-in values for luxury watches, making it easier to acquire your next dream timepiece.

Investment Advisory

Luxury watches can be exceptional investments. Our team provides expert guidance on market trends and helps you build a collection with long-term value appreciation.
