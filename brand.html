Luxury Watch Brands

Discover the world's most prestigious watchmakers

The Pinnacle of Horology

We proudly represent the most distinguished names in luxury watchmaking. Each brand we carry has earned its reputation through decades, sometimes centuries, of exceptional craftsmanship and innovation.

Rolex

Switzerland | Founded 1905

The crown of watchmaking excellence. Rolex has set the standard for luxury timepieces with iconic models like the Submariner, Daytona, and Datejust. Known for precision, durability, and timeless design.

Iconic Design Investment Value COSC Certified

Patek Philippe

Switzerland | Founded 1839

The ultimate expression of haute horlogerie. Patek Philippe creates some of the world's most complicated and valuable timepieces. Each watch represents generations of Swiss watchmaking tradition and innovation.

Haute Horlogerie Complications Heirloom Quality

Audemars Piguet

Switzerland | Founded 1875

Avant-garde Swiss craftsmanship. Famous for the revolutionary Royal Oak, Audemars Piguet combines bold design with exceptional mechanical innovation. A symbol of contemporary luxury.

Iconic Royal Oak Bold Design Limited Production

Omega

Switzerland | Founded 1848

Precision timing excellence. As the official timekeeper of the Olympics and the first watch on the moon, Omega combines sporting heritage with luxury craftsmanship. The Speedmaster and Seamaster are true icons.

Moonwatch Co-Axial Movement Olympic Heritage

IWC Schaffhausen

Switzerland | Founded 1868

Engineering excellence meets Swiss precision. IWC is renowned for its pilot watches and Portuguese collection. Each timepiece showcases technical innovation and masculine elegance.

Pilot Watches Engineering Focus In-House Movements

Cartier

France | Founded 1847

Luxury and elegance redefined. Cartier brings Parisian sophistication to horology with iconic models like the Tank and Santos. Known for distinctive design and exceptional finishing.

Jewelry Heritage Distinctive Design Tank Collection
